About Virat

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is one of the most celebrated cricketers in the history of the sport. Often hailed as a modern-day legend, Kohli is renowned not only for his remarkable batting skills but also for his fierce competitiveness, discipline, and leadership qualities.

Off the field, Kohli is a global icon with millions of followers and major endorsements. His marriage to actress Anushka Sharma adds to his public profile. He also runs the Virat Kohli Foundation which supports underprivileged children and promotes sports development.

Achievements

  • ICC Cricketer of the Year – 2017, 2018
  • Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna – 2018
  • Padma Shri – 2017
  • Fastest ODI century by an Indian (52 balls)
  • Led India to their first Test series win in Australia (2018–19)

Records

  • Fastest to 8000, 9000, 10000, 11000, and 12000 ODI runs
  • Most centuries in successful ODI chases
  • Highest rating points for an Indian batsman in ICC ODI rankings – 911
  • Most double hundreds as an Indian Test captain (7)
